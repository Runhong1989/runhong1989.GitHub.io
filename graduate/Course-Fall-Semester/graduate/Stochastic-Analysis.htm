Stochastic Analysis

The course is designed for senior undergraduate students and graduate students. Topics covered include: Martingales (Semimartingales), Stopping Times and Filtrations, Brownian Motion and Poisson Process, (General) Stochastic Integration, Ito formula and Representation of Martingales, Girsanovâ€™s Theorem, Local times, Stochastic Differential Equations (SDE), Markov Processes and Levy Theory, Applications to PDE, and Stochastic control and Insurance Mathematics as well as Mathematical Finance.

Textbooks
