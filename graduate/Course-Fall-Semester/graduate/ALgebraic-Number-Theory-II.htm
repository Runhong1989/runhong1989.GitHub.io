Algebraic Number Theory - II

This is a continuation of the course “Algebraic Number Theory I” for senior undergraduate students and graduate students. it consists of class field theory, Tate's thesis and Chebotarev density theorem.

Prerequisite
Point set topology, representation of finite groups.

Textbooks

  1. Algebraic Number Theory (ed. by J. Cassels and A. Frohlich) , Academic Press: London and New York, 1967.
  2. S. Lang, Algebraic Number Theory, Addison-Wesley, 1970.
  3. H. Davenport, Multiplicative number theory, GTM 74.
  4. 加藤和也, 黑川信重, 斋藤毅. 数论 I--Fermat 的梦想和类域论, 高等教育出版社, 胥鸣伟, 印林生译.
  5. D. A. Cox, Primes of the form x2+ny2: Fermat, Class Field Theory, and Complex Multiplication, 2nd Edition, John Wiley & Sons, 2013.
