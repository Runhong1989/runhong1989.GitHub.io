Partial Differential Equations - II

This course is designed for graduate students and senior undergraduate students. The contents cover all of the textbook “Partial Differential Equations Part II”, by Craig Evans. Besides, we will try to add the followings:

  1. The imbedding theorems for the general Sobolev space W^{m,p}(\Omega)；
  2. The compact imbedding theorems for the general Sobolev space W^{m,p}(\Omega);
  3. The Schauder estimates for linear elliptic equations;
  4. The weak maximum principle and De Giorgi iteration for linear elliptic equations;
  5. Viscosity solutions, Alexandroff maximum principle and Harnack inequality for linear elliptic equations.

Textbooks

  1. L C Evans, Partial Differential Equations, Part II, AMS, 1998.

References

  1. Elliptic partial differential equations, AMS, 2000.
  2. D Gilbarg and N S Trudinger, Elliptic partial differential equations of second order, Springer, 1998.
  3. R. A. Adams and J. J. F. Fournier, Sobolev spaces, Academic press, 2003.

Prerequisites
数学分析 (mathematical analysis)，实分析 (real analysis)，泛函分析初步.
