Abstract Algebra-II

This is an introductory course designed for undergraduates and beginning graduated students who have known some basic facts about groups, rings, fields and Galois theory.

After quickly reviewing field extensions and Galois theory with a succinct proof in two weeks, we begins to introduce the theory of modules. The topics covered in the course include basic commutative algebra like tensor product, left (right) exact functors and localization, and so on; homological algebra such as the definitions of derived functors and cohomology of groups; and the representations of finite groups based on the classification of semisimple algebras.
