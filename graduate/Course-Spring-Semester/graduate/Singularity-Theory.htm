Singularity Theory

The students are expected to do the presentation of the two reference books. I shall go through some topics in singularity theory including

  1. Resolution of plane curve singularities.
  2. Resolution of singularities of two dimensional analytic spaces.
  3. Normalization of two dimensional analytic spaces.
  4. Exceptional sets.
  5. Minimal resolutions.
  6. Equivalence embeddings, Space with nilpotents, cohomology in sheaves of non-abelian groups, Formal isomorphisms, Formal equivalence implies actual equivalence.
  7. Local ring structure.

Reference books

  1. Robert C. Gunning, Introduction to holomorphic functions of several complex variables, Vol. II: Local Theory.
  2. Robert C. Gunning, Introduction to holomorphic functions of several complex variables, Vol. III: Homology Theory.
