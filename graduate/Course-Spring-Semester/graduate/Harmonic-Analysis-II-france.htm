Harmonic Analysis -- Singular Integrals

Topics of the course include

  1. Recalls on Fourier transform and convolution.
  2. The Hardy-Littlewood maximal operator and the Lebesgue derivation theorem.
  3. Principal values â€“ Some Fourier transforms.
  4. The Calderon-Zygmund theory. Hilbert and Riesz transforms.
  5. The Littlewood-Paley theory.
  6. Higher Riesz transforms. Spherical harmonics. Non-smooth kernels.
  7. BMO and H^1.
  8. Interpolation: The Marcinkiewicz interpolation theorem. The Riesz-Thorin interpolation theorem. Real and complex interpolation with BMO.
