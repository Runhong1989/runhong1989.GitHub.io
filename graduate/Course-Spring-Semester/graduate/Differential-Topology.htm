Differential Topology

This course is to provide an elementary introduction to differential topology. The topics covered include definitions and basic properties of manifolds and submanifolds; transversality; Sard's theorem; the Frobenius theorem; the Whitney embedding theorem; mod 2 intersection theory with applications to the Jordan-Brouwer separation theorem and the Borsuk-Ulam theorem; oriented intersection theory, the Poincare-Hopf theorem, the Lefschetz fixed-point theorem and the Hopf degree theorem; differential forms and integration; the Stokes theorem and the Gauss-Bonnet theorem.

Textbooks

  1. V. Guillemin, A. Pollack, Differential Topology.
  2. S.S. Chern, W.H. Chen, Lecture on Differential Geometry (only Chapter 1)
