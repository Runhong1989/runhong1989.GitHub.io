Advanced Probability

Plan of the course

Chapter I. Martingales

  1. Conditional expectation.
  2. Martingales.
  3. Discrete stochastic integration.
  4. Convergences.
  5. Applications to branching processes.
  6. Uniform integrability.

Chapter II. Content to be announced

Chapter III. Markov Chains

  1. Definitions and Markov property.
  2. Recurrence, transience.
  3. Stationary measures and ergodic theorems.
  4. Applications.

Chapter IV. Content to be announced.

Chapter V. Brownian motion

  1. Construction.
  2. Markov property.
  3. Sample paths.
  4. Donsker’s theorem.

Chapter VI. Content to be announced.

Chapters II, IV and VI are not part of the program in the examinations. Each of these chapters gives a quick glance at a research topic.
