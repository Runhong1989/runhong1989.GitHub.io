Analysis

Course Contents

Chapter 0. Some Further Results for Lp Spaces

  1. Compactness of sets in Lp spaces.
  2. Weak compactness criterion for L1 space: Dunford-Pettis theorem.

Chapter 1. Rearrangement Inequalities

  1. Rearrangements of sets and functions.
  2. Riesz's rearrangement inequality: compactness argument and compet- ing symmetries.
  3. Steiner symmetrization.

Chapter 2. Integral Inequalities

  1. Young's inequality.
  2. Hardy-Littlewood-Sobolev inequality.
  3. Conformal transformations and stereographic projection.
  4. Conformal invariance of the Hardy-Littlewood-Sobolev inequality.
  5. Competing symmetries.
  6. Sharp version of Hardy-Littlewood-Sobolev inequality.

Chapter 3. Fourier Transforms

  1. Definition of the L1 Fourier transform.
  2. Fourier transform of a Gaussian, Plancherel's theorem.
  3. Definition of the L2 Fourier transform.
  4. Inversion formula.
  5. The Fourier transform in Lp(RN ).
  6. The sharp Hausdorff-Young inequality.

Chapter 4. Sobolev Inequalities

  1. Spaces H1 and H1/2.
  2. Sobolev inequalities for Wm,p(Omega), Rellich-Kondrashov theorem.
  3. Poincare's inequality for Wm,p(Omega).
  4. Nash's inequality.
  5. The logarithmic Sobolev inequality.
  6. Heat kernel and contraction semigroups.
  7. Equivalence of Nash's inequality and smoothing estimates, application to the heat equation.
  8. Derivation of the heat kernel via logarithmic Sobolev inequalities.

Chapter 5. Introduction to the Calculous of Variations

  1. The direct method in the calculous of variations.
  2. Example 1: Time independent Schordinger equation and the energy function.
  3. Existence of the minimizer.
  4. Regularity and uniqueness of the minimizers.
  5. Example 2: The Thomas-Fermi problem.
    6. Existence of an unconstrained Thomas-Fermi minimizer. The Thomas-Fermi equation and the Thomas-Fermi minimizer with constrain.
  6. Example 3: The capacitor problem, capacity. Solutions of the capacitor problem.

Textbook
E. Lieb and M. Loss, Analysis.

Prerequisites
Real analysis, functional analysis, basic PDE knowledge.
