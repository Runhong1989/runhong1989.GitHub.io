Basic Homotopy Theory with some applications to Differential Topology

The goal of the course is to do Serre's work on homotopy groups of spheres through his spectral sequence and by thus developing his mod C homotopy theory as done in the last chapter of Spanier's Algebraic Topology text. Applications would be the Thom-Hirzebruch Index formula (at least in dimensions 4 and 8) leading to Milnor's example of a manifold homeomorphic but not diffeomorphic to the 7 dimensional sphere. Before doing these, basic results like the Hurewicz theorem, Whitehead theorem and Freudenthal suspension theorem would be proven.
