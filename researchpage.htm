I work in the field of mathematical physics and am interested in algebraic and geometric aspects of quantum field theory and string theory.

Expositions

  • Effective Batalin-Vilkovisky quantization and geometric applications, arXiv:1709.00669[math.QA]
    ----This is an exposition on the effective renormalization of quantum field theory in the Batalin-Vilkovisky formalism. We illustrate its mathematical use by three geometric applications.
  • Calabi-Yau geometry, primitive form and mirror symmetry , available here
    ----This note explains the connection between K.Saito's theory of primitive forms and B-model Kodaira-Spencer theory on Calabi-Yau geometry.
  • Some classical/quantum aspects of Calabi-Yau moduli. available here
    ---- This note explains the basic idea of quantization of Calabi-Yau geometry and higher genus B-model.
  • Simi-infinite Hodge structures: from BCOV theory to Seiberg-Witten geometry , available here
    ----talk at String-Math 2018.
